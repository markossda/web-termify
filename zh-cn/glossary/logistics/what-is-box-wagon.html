箱式货车 是什么？

箱式货车 A rail freight wagon with a fully enclosed structure, used for transporting weather-sensitive general cargo; referenced in rail waybills and UIC documentation.

Source: Incoterms 2020 (ICC), FIATA, IATA DGR, WCO

How is “箱式货车” Used in Practice?

箱式货车装载了消费品用于铁路运输，并记录在铁路运单中。
