债务收入比 是什么？

债务收入比 A personal finance measure that compares an individual's total monthly debt payments to their gross monthly income, used by lenders to assess repayment capacity and credit risk.

Source: CFA Institute, IFRS Foundation, FASB (GAAP), Basel III Framework
