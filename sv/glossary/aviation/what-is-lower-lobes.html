What is Lower lobes?

Lower lobes The compartments in the lower fuselage of an aircraft, often used for cargo or, on some aircraft, additional fuel storage.

Source: ICAO Doc 9432, FAA PCG

How is “Lower lobes” Used in Practice?

Cargo is loaded into the lower lobes before passengers board the aircraft.
