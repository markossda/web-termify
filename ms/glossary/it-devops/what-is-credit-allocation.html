What is Credit Allocation?

Credit Allocation Credit allocation is the operational assignment of usage credits or spending limits to specific cloud accounts, projects, or users, as defined in platform billing systems.

Source: ITIL v4, AWS Well-Architected Framework, Kubernetes Documentation, CNCF

How is “Credit Allocation” Used in Practice?

Credit allocation allows administrators to control spend by assigning cloud credits to specific departments or projects.
