Was ist House Bill?

House Bill A Bill of Lading issued by a freight forwarder to a shipper, covering cargo consolidated in a master consignment.

Source: Incoterms 2020 (ICC), FIATA, IATA DGR, WCO

How is “House Bill” Used in Practice?

Der Spediteur stellte dem Exporteur ein House Bill für 20 Paletten unter einem Master Bill aus.
