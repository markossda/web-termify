Apa itu Kebijakan Karantina Endpoint?

Kebijakan Karantina Endpoint A defined set of rules for isolating endpoints that exhibit suspicious or non-compliant behavior to prevent them from accessing sensitive systems or networks until remediated.

Source: ISO 27001, NIST Cybersecurity Framework, MITRE ATT&CK

How is “Kebijakan Karantina Endpoint” Used in Practice?

Setelah mendeteksi malware, kebijakan karantina endpoint secara otomatis mengisolasi laptop yang terkena dampak dari jaringan perusahaan.
