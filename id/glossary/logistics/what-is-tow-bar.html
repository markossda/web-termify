Apa itu Batang penarik?

Batang penarik A rigid bar used for towing vehicles or trailers, providing a secure, direct mechanical connection between the towing vehicle and the towed unit; subject to ECE R55, SAE J684, and IATA AHM guidelines for airside use.

Source: Incoterms 2020 (ICC), FIATA, IATA DGR, WCO

How is “Batang penarik” Used in Practice?

Batang penarik harus kompatibel dengan kendaraan penarik dan peralatan yang ditarik, sesuai manual teknis operator armada.
