ما هو إدارة مخاطر الإرهاق؟

إدارة مخاطر الإرهاق A systematic approach by operators to monitor and manage crew fatigue risk, including procedures, training, and reporting, in compliance with ICAO and EASA regulations (ICAO Doc 9966).

Source: ICAO Doc 9432, FAA PCG
