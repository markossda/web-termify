What is Protective breathing equipment?

Protective breathing equipment A certified, self-contained smoke hood that provides crew with filtered air for a limited time in the presence of smoke or toxic fumes.

Source: ICAO Doc 9432, FAA PCG

How is “Protective breathing equipment” Used in Practice?

Cabin crew donned protective breathing equipment and entered lavatory to address smoke warning; communication maintained throughout intervention.
