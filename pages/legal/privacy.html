Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 23, 2026

1. Introduction

BipBoo Corporation ("we," "us," or "our") operates the Termify mobile application (the "App"), a 100% free professional English learning app currently available on iOS. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use the Termify App.

By downloading or using Termify, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with the terms of this Privacy Policy, please do not access or use the App.

2. Information We Collect

Termify is designed with a privacy-first approach. We practice minimal data collection and do not sell your personal data to third parties.

2.1 Information You Provide

Termify does not require account creation or login. We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, or any other personally identifiable information unless you voluntarily contact us at our support email.

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

When you use the App, we may automatically collect limited, non-personally identifiable information, including:

  • Device type and operating system version
  • App version number
  • General usage statistics (such as which features are used most frequently)
  • Crash logs and performance diagnostics
  • Language and region settings

This information is collected in aggregate form and cannot be used to identify individual users.

2.3 Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Personal names, email addresses, or phone numbers (unless you contact us)
  • Location data (GPS or precise location)
  • Contacts, photos, or files from your device
  • Payment or financial information (the App is 100% free)
  • Browsing history or activity outside the App

3. How We Use Information

The limited information we collect is used exclusively for the following purposes:

  • App improvement: Understanding which features and industry modules (Aviation, Maritime, Logistics, Finance, Cybersecurity, IT/DevOps) are most popular to prioritize development efforts
  • Bug fixes and stability: Using crash reports and diagnostics to identify and resolve technical issues
  • Analytics: Measuring aggregate usage patterns to improve the learning experience
  • Content optimization: Improving the quality and coverage of the 4,230+ professional English terms across all supported industries

We do not use your information for targeted advertising, profiling, or any purpose unrelated to improving the Termify App.

4. Data Storage and Security

We take the security of your information seriously and implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect data against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

  • All data transmitted between the App and our servers is encrypted using industry-standard TLS (Transport Layer Security) protocols
  • Aggregated analytics data is stored on secure, access-controlled servers
  • We retain automatically collected data only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described in this Privacy Policy
  • Your learning progress and preferences are stored locally on your device and are not transmitted to our servers

5. Third-Party Services

Termify may use the following third-party services, each governed by their own privacy policies:

  • Apple App Store: Current distribution platform that may collect information according to its own privacy policy
  • Analytics providers: We may use privacy-respecting analytics services to understand aggregate App usage. These services process data in compliance with applicable data protection regulations
  • Crash reporting: We may use crash reporting services to improve App stability. These services receive only technical diagnostic data and do not receive personally identifiable information

We do not share, sell, rent, or trade your personal information with third parties for their commercial purposes.

6. Children's Privacy

Termify is rated 4+ on the App Store and is suitable for users of all ages. The App does not contain objectionable content, in-app purchases, or advertising.

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. Since Termify does not require registration or login and does not collect personally identifiable information, the App is safe for use by minors with parental guidance.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe that your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us at support@protermify.com and we will promptly delete such information.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have certain rights regarding your data, including:

  • The right to access any data we hold about you
  • The right to request deletion of your data
  • The right to opt out of analytics data collection
  • The right to data portability

Since Termify collects minimal, non-personally identifiable data, most of these rights are inherently satisfied. If you have any requests or questions regarding your data rights, please contact us at support@protermify.com.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make changes, we will revise the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the App after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.

We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically to stay informed about how we protect your information.
