O que e Acesso Just-In-Time?

Acesso Just-In-Time A privileged access management method that grants users temporary, time-bound, and auditable access rights to critical systems only when needed, reducing the risk of standing privileges.

Source: ISO 27001, NIST Cybersecurity Framework, MITRE ATT&CK

How is “Acesso Just-In-Time” Used in Practice?

Conceda acesso just-in-time ao servidor de banco de dados para o administrador realizar patch de emergência, revogando automaticamente as credenciais após o uso.
